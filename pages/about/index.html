About me

Hi! I am a currently in vacancy, after a few years working as an optimization engineer for Artelys, France.

I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, mainly in the Julia language. In particular, I am a member of the JuliaOpt community and the current maintainer of the open-source package Knitro.jl.

Before that, I was between 2015 and 2018 a PhD candidate in mathematical optimization at CERMICS. The goal of my thesis was to apply decomposition/coordination methods in a stochastic setting, to find the optimal control of large-scale energy systems. The manuscript of the thesis is publicly available here, and the slides of my PhD defense are available here.

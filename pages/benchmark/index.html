Optimization benchmarks

With my collaborators, we are maintaining various benchmarks for optimization solvers:

  • COPSBenchmark.jl: a JuMP implementation of the cops benchmark originally developped at Argonne National Laboratory.

  • MPCCBenchmark.jl: a JuMP implementation of various large-scale mathematical programs with complementarity constraints, featuring non-smooth optimal control instances, equilibrium problems and power system problems.

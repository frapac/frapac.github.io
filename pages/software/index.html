Software Projects

I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, mainly in Julia. In particular, I am a member of the JuMP and the exanauts communities.

MadSuite

MadSuite is a suite of GPU-accelerated optimization solvers, featuring:

  • MadNLP, a solver for nonlinear programming;

  • MadIPM, a solver for linear programming;

  • MadNCL, a solver for degenerate nonlinear programming and problems with complementarity constraints.

For more information, go check out the MadSuite website!
